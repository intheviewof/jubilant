# Streaming
## Streaming Choices
> [!IMPORTANT]
> The format you may get depends on the song, and the artist uploading the song. Some artists are known to upload fake lossless files.

- 🏆 **[Tidal](https://tidal.com/)** - Streaming / Super Hi-Res FLAC / Dolby Atmos
- 🏆 **[Apple Music](https://music.apple.com/us/new)** - Streaming / Super Hi-Res FLAC / Dolby Atmos / Great availability
- 🏆 **[Bandcamp](https://bandcamp.com/)** - Purchase / Download / Super Hi-Res FLAC / Pick your own format
- 🌟 **[Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/)** - Streaming / Free: 160kbps OGG / Premium: 320kbps OGG / Great availability / Use Adblock
- 🌟 **[Deezer](https://www.deezer.com/us/)** - Streaming / Free: 128kbps MP3 / Premium: Lossless FLAC
- **[Qobuz](https://www.qobuz.com)** - Streaming / Purchase / Super Hi-Res FLAC
